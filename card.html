Tiger

Being the largest cat spieces, the strip patterns are unique to each tiger.

    Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
    Average Length: 3.5m
    Average Weight: 187.5Kgs
    Habitat: Eastern Eurasia

Tigers have muscular bodies with powerful forelimbs, large heads and long tails. The pelage is dense and heavy; colouration varies between shades of orange and brown with white ventral areas and distinctive vertical black stripes, whose patterns are unique to each individual.
